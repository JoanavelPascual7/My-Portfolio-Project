Recommendations

Here are some of Joanavel Pascual's top recommendations for places to visit in New York City:

  • Central Park: A beautiful oasis in the heart of the city, Central Park is perfect for picnics, bike rides, or just a leisurely stroll.
  • The Metropolitan Museum of Art: One of the world's greatest art museums, the Met has a vast collection spanning thousands of years and cultures.
  • Times Square: The iconic "Crossroads of the World," Times Square is a bustling hub of activity and entertainment.
